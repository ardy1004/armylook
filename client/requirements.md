## Packages
framer-motion | Smooth scroll animations and section reveals for a premium feel
embla-carousel-react | Required for the interactive image gallery/carousel
lucide-react | Icons for property features and UI elements
react-hook-form | Form state management
@hookform/resolvers | Form validation integration
zod | Schema validation

## Notes
- Smooth scrolling is heavily used to guide users from CTAs to the lead form.
- The form submits a lead via POST /api/leads and then immediately redirects to a pre-filled WhatsApp URL on success.
- External images from Cloudinary are used as requested.
